About Us

Koko AI is a cutting-edge platform that seamlessly transforms submitted form data into a fully operational calling assistant. By leveraging advanced AI technology, Koko AI automates the creation process, turning the information provided in a simple form submission into a personalized and responsive assistant. This system is particularly useful for businesses that need quick and efficient communication solutions. With the added capability of integrating with services like Twilio, Koko AI ensures that each assistant is assigned an available phone number, making the setup process both streamlined and user-friendly.
